About Map

Brawl stars maps are usually created by the development team however many players have decided to try create their own and if impressive enough can be noticed by the dev team where they will officially make it a map with the original creators name being credited. These maps usually never last forever though and are regularly switched out to try maintain a new perspective of the game. The more played game modes such as brawl ball, showdown and duo showdown will have the most maps however the modes which have the least amount of maps are the special game modes.
