About

Characters in Brawl Stars are known as brawlers. Each brawler has unique abilities as well as advantages and weaknesses when compared to other brawlers. Many balance changes are implemented as a ways to balance the game and make sure no brawler is too good or too bad. There are currently 72 brawlers in the game and the META (most effective tactics available) in this case are the brawlers which are at the top meaning they are usually better than most other brawlers. This always changes as balance changes are quite frequent so it is possible for a very good brawler to suddenly fall out of the meta or for a bad brawler to suddenly become part of the META.

Each brawler has a basic attack which players can always use as long as they have ammo which is shown on the top. They also have a special ability which is known as a "Super" which can be used once this bar is charged. It can become game changing if used correctly. Each brawler also has 2 gadgets which help the brawler such as extra speed, health or even better sight. Finally, there are some brawlers which have a super charge system which is a new game changing element in the game. This allows certain brawlers to increase in damage, speed and their super will now have an extra effect whether it may be extra range, damage or size.

Brawlers can be put into a class depending on their play style, abilities and health. These categories are:

  • Damage dealers - brawlers which are aggressive and do a lot of dps (damage per second)
  • Tanks - High health brawlers who can charge the enemy as well as providing cover and taunting for your own team
  • Marksman - Snipers who usually play passively aggressive trying to do heavy damage whilst staying at the back
  • Artillery - Brawlers who throw their projectiles
  • Controller - Passive play style who try control the game by applying pressure and not allowing enemies to break through
  • Assassins - All assassins have different play styles and are great at 1v1's. They also help your team push up and be more aggressive
  • Support - Brawlers who are great at helping the team out whether its by healing or providing boosts or shields now for brawlers
